How It Works

Whithout Interceptor

When you use a standard dApp, it connects directly to MetaMask, which generates transaction signatures that are often complex and hard to understand

Indecisiveness can put you at risk, especially if you're not aware on how blockchain transactions work

With Interceptor

The Interceptor sits between the dApp and MetaMask, stepping in to "intercept" transaction requests before they go through. This enables Interceptor to provide clear simulation of a transaction, along with built-in protections.

A better insight on transactions allows you to make more informed decisions
